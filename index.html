In A Mood?

When tough times made their rounds, I've always immeresed myself into those classic inspirational videos, or thoughtfully written quotes. I found them beneficial and supportive seeing that they can rapidly change your perspective; specifically in lessons that can sometimes take a lifetime to grasp.

Below is a versatile collection of ultra-modern, "old-head", and consciously driven inspired quotes - some of the most heart-warming, but hard-bitten words, voiced by the most iconic. I hope that you enjoy, and can replenish yourself through inspiration and wisdom.

"Even when you're fed up, you've got to keep your head up." - Tupac Shakur

Anime

"I will be hokage, everyone will ahve to stop disrespecting me and look up to me. Believe it!!" - Naruto Uzumaki

Anime has constantly fufilled and supported me in times of loneliness. Growing up self-supporting and in foster homes, anime is what kept me feeling lively. In foster homes, I was alienated and felt as though I was in an asylum. With anime, I felt whole again.
